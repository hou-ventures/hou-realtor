How it works

Whether you're a person or an AI agent, the flow is the same.

Step 1

Find the property

Use Zillow, Redfin, HAR, or any source. Your AI assistant can search for you. Take your time — just don't sign a buyer's agreement with another agent or broker.

Step 2

Send us the details

Property address, offer price, financing, and contingencies. Email us or (soon) submit via API. Our AI drafts the TREC contract. Tiffany Hua, our licensed agent, reviews it.

Step 3

Offer submitted

We submit the offer to the listing agent on your behalf. You get full licensed buyer representation from offer through close. $49. Done.

Important: Do not sign a buyer's agreement or exclusive contract with any other real estate agent or broker before contacting us. Under Texas law, you have the right to choose your representation.
